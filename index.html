Preliminary Results of the Local Government Elections


Whilst the proceedings relative to recounts in four electoral districts is underway, these are the preliminary results of the Local Government Elections.

On behalf of the Royal Elections Commission, we want to congratulate Trecay Darai on the campaign they have run so far. You have conducted yourself with dignity and discipline while articulating your vision and goals for this community.
We eagerly anticipate your continued engagement in molding this community’s future. We encourage Trecay Darai to view the complete listing of the results available on the commission’s website.
